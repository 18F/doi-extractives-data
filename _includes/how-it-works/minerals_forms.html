Revenue collected by the Bureau of Land Management and the Office of Natural Resources Revenue

Claim-staking Fees

Processing Fee $20, Location Fee $37, Initial Maintenance Fee $155.

On acquired lands, miners must pay a one-time $6,500 Prospecting Permit Fee.

Annual Fees

Annual Maintenance Fee $155.

Bonus

No bonuses are paid for locatable hardrock mining.

Rent

Miners only pay annual fees and rent on acquired lands.

Prospecting Fee $0.50 per acre. Rent $1.00 per acre.

Royalty

No royalties are paid for locatable hardrock mining. Mining hardrock minerals on acquired land is exempt from minimum production and royalty requirements under Title 43 in the Code of Federal Regulations.
