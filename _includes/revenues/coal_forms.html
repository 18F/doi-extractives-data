Revenue collected by the Bureau of Land Management and the Office of Natural Resources Revenue

Bonus

A bonus is the amount paid by the highest bidder for a natural resource lease.

Rent

$3.00 per acre or a fraction thereof.

Rent is an annual payment for leasing lands or waters before production starts.

Royalty

12.5% surface mining, 8% subsurface mining.

A royalty is a payment for the natural resources extracted, determined by a percentage of the resourcesâ€™ production value. For federal coal, the rate depends on whether the coal was extracted by surface or subsurface mining.

Abandoned Mine Land Fee

$0.28 per ton for surface mining, $0.12 per ton for subsurface mining.

A fee for current day coal production that funds reclamation of mines abandoned prior to 1977.
