Revenue collected by the Bureau of Land Management and the Office of Natural Resources Revenue

Bonus

The bonus is the amount paid by the highest bidder for a natural resource lease on federal land.

Rent

$1.50 per acre for five years
$2.00 per acre thereafter

Rent is an annual payment made on leased land, for the exclusive right to apply to explore for and develop natural resources on federal land for future production.

Royalty

12.5%

A royalty is a payment determined by the percentage of the market value of the produced commodity.
