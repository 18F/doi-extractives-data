Renewables: Onshore

Onshore renewable energy development on federal lands includes solar, wind, and other renewable resources. Title V of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 provides the Department of the Interior’s Bureau of Land Management (BLM) with the authority to issue rights-of-way for developing solar and wind energy projects on federal lands.
