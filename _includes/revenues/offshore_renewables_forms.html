Revenue collected by the Office of Natural Resources Revenue

Bonus

The amount the highest bidder paid for a natural resource lease.

Acquisition Fee

A fee charged for securing an uncompetitive lease in place of a bonus.

$0.25 per acre

Rent

An annual payment for leasing lands or waters.

$3.00 per acre

Operating Fee

A revenue payment for a percentage (2% unless otherwise specified or waived) of the anticipated value of the wind energy produced on federal waters.
