Revenue collected by the Office of Natural Resources Revenue

Bonus

A bonus is the amount paid by the highest bidder for a natural resource lease.

Rent

Offshore either $7.00 or $11.00 per acre depending on depth, increasing throughout the term of the lease up to $44.00 per acre in some cases.

Rent is an annual payment for leasing lands or waters before production starts. Rent is set in the lease.
