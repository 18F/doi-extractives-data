Revenue collected by the Bureau of Land Management

Bonus

The bonus is the amount paid by the highest bidder for a natural resource lease on federal land.

Rent

Rent is an annual payment made on leased land for the right to continue exploration and development of federal land for future production. Under the proposed rule, rent is determined by the acre and is based on land values.

MC Fee

$6,209 per MW for wind or $3,548 – $5,322 per MW for solar

A megawatt capacity fee (or MC fee) is payment based on the value of electricity generated. These fees are still under discussion and may change.
