Revenue collected by the Bureau of Land Management

Note: proposed revenue terms are more favorable for development within designated leasing areas.

Bonus

The amount the highest bidder paid for a natural resource lease.

Rent

An annual payment for leasing lands or waters. Under the proposed rule, rent is determined by the acre and based on land values.

Megawatt Capacity Fee (MC Fee)

A revenue payment for the calculated value of electricity generated on federal lands.
