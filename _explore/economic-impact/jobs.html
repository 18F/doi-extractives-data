Jobs

Explore extractive industries jobs from 2004 to 2013 by state for both salaried employees and self-employed.

There are two types of jobs data here. Wage and Salary data is from the Bureau of Labor Statistics and describes the number of people employed in natural resource extraction that receive wages or salaries from companies. Self-Employed data is from the Bureau of Economic Analysis and describes people who work in natural resource extraction, but don’t receive wages or salaries because they own their company.

Note: The Self-Employed data includes some double counting because people appear on multiple tax forms.
