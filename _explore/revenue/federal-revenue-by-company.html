Federal Revenue by Company

In 2013, the federal government collected over $9.8 billion in royalties and over 2 billion in bonuses from resources extracted on federal lands. Some of the companies that paid the most in royalties, bonuses, and other fees include Chevron Corporation, Shell, BP America Inc., and Exxon Mobile Corporation.
