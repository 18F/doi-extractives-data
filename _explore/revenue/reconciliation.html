Reconciliation

During the USEITI process, companies report payments to the government (such as rents, taxes, and royalties), and those reports are compared with government records of revenue received.

The 2015 report looked at calendar year data for 2013. The independent administrator identified and investigated 17 material variances.

The 45 companies invited to participate are listed here, along with reported payments and reconciliation results for each revenue type.
