About this site

In support of the U.S. implementation of the international Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative, the Department of the Interior (DOI) is providing this public data portal that tells the story of natural resource revenues from Federal lands. This website aligns with the principles of Project Open Data and the goals of the U.S. National Action Plan for the Open Government Partnership.
